About Us

The MENZ CLASSY Story

2018

Founded in Kigali

Redefining Men's Grooming in Rwanda

MENZ CLASSY was born from a passion for craftsmanship and a vision to elevate men's grooming standards in Rwanda. What started as a single-chair barbershop has grown into Kigali's premier destination for discerning gentlemen seeking exceptional service and style.

Our founder, a master barber with international experience, recognized the need for a space where tradition meets contemporary style, where every client receives personalized attention and leaves feeling confident.

Premium Products: We use only the finest grooming products from trusted international brands.

Sanitation Excellence: All tools are sterilized between clients for your safety and peace of mind.

Community Hub: More than a barbershop, we're a gathering place for meaningful conversations.
